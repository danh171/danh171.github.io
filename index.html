I'm a software developer living in Dublin, Ireland. I have a BSc. in Applied Physics from Dublin City University, and a MSc. in Computer Science from University College Dublin.

I began my professional career in the software industry at a startup company called Area22, developing a recommender system in Python. Towards the end of my time at UCD, I managed to secure an internship as a developer at Groupon, working on an internal ETL tool written in Java. My most recent position was as a developer at Susquehanna International Group, at which I developed a variety of internal tooling using mainly Python and .NET.

My professional programming languages of choice over the years have been Python and Java. In my personal time I've become fairly proficient in C and, more recently, have begun to focus on learning the basics of some full-stack web frameworks.

In March of 2024, I decided to take some time out to do some travelling and spend time with family. I'm back on the job hunt now, and have made this website to act as an archive for all of the different technologies I hope to learn in the future. Hopefully these blog posts will be helpful to somebody out there.

Cheers! 🍻
